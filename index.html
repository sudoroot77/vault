Common Inquiries Our FAQ

1. What types of assets can I store at Faxguard Vault ?

We offer storage solutions for a wide range of assets, including gold, silver, platinum, and other precious metals, as well as rare coins and other collectibles.

2. How secure is my asset with Faxguard Vault?

At Faxguard Vault, we take security very seriously. Our facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art security measures, including biometric access control systems, 24/7 video surveillance, and armed guards.

3. Can I access my assets at any time?

Yes, you can access your assets during our regular business hours, which are Monday to Friday, 9 am to 5 pm. However, we encourage you to schedule an appointment in advance to ensure that we have your assets ready for you when you arrive.

4. How do I withdraw my assets from Faxguard Vault?

To withdraw your assets, simply log in to your account on our website and submit a withdrawal request. We will then prepare your assets for pickup or arrange for them to be shipped to your designated location.

5. How much does it cost to store my assets at Faxguard ?

Our storage fees are competitive and based on the size of your deposit box or the value of your assets. Please contact us for more information on our pricing.

We hope that these questions and answers have been helpful. If you have any further questions or concerns, please don’t hesitate to contact us or visit our FAQ page for more information.
